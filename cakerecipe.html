Our Cake Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake made with cocoa powder and dark chocolate.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.

Vanilla Cake

A classic vanilla sponge cake with a fluffy texture.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Fold in dry ingredients and bake for 30 minutes.

Red Velvet Cake

Delicious red velvet cake with a rich cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp red food coloring
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix wet and dry ingredients separately.
  3. Combine and bake for 30 minutes.

Lemon Cake

A zesty lemon cake with a tangy lemon glaze.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix ingredients and bake for 30 minutes.
  3. Drizzle with lemon glaze after baking.

Carrot Cake

A moist and spiced carrot cake topped with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix ingredients and bake for 30 minutes.
  3. Top with cream cheese frosting.
