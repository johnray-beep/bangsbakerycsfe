Our Bread Recipes

Sourdough Bread

A tangy and chewy bread made with a natural yeast starter.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
Instructions:
  1. Mix ingredients and let dough rise for 12 hours.
  2. Shape and bake at 450°F (230°C) for 30 minutes.

Baguette

A classic French bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 cup water
Instructions:
  1. Mix ingredients and let rise for 2 hours.
  2. Shape into baguettes and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 25 minutes.

Whole Wheat Bread

A healthy and fiber-rich bread made with whole wheat flour.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water
Instructions:
  1. Mix ingredients and let rise for 2 hours.
  2. Shape into a loaf and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.

Focaccia Bread

An Italian flatbread topped with olive oil and herbs.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Instructions:
  1. Mix ingredients and let dough rise for 1 hour.
  2. Flatten, top with oil and herbs, and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes.

Banana Bread

A sweet and moist bread made with ripe bananas.

Ingredients:
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
Instructions:
  1. Mash bananas and mix with other ingredients.
  2. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 40 minutes.
