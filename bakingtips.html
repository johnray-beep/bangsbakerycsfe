Baking Tips

1. Measure Ingredients Accurately

Use a kitchen scale for precise measurements, especially for flour and sugar.

2. Use Room Temperature Ingredients

Bringing butter, eggs, and milk to room temperature helps ingredients mix better.

3. Preheat Your Oven

Ensure your oven is fully preheated before placing your batter inside for even baking.

4. Don’t Overmix the Batter

Overmixing can make cakes dense and tough. Mix until just combined.

5. Test for Doneness Properly

Use a toothpick to check if your cake is baked—if it comes out clean, it’s done.
